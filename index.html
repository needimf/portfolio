About Me

I’m a Full Stack Engineer at PayClearly LLC working in JavaScript, React, Firebase, and other Google Cloud Services. Additionally, I have experience with the MERN Stack, Ruby on Rails, Python, HTML and CSS.

As one of the early team members at PayClearly, I have been a key player in the growth of our product over the past 3 years. Since I arrived, our payment processing has increased from tens to thousands per month, our product has evolved from a virtual-card payments interface into a suite of Accounts Payable (AP) services, and our team has more than doubled in size. I’ve built features; established engineering best-practices; communicated with clients to understand their needs; hired, trained and led employees; and even started our can recycling program. Most recently, I refactored our front-end into a monorepo, and implemented a new server and deployment strategy to enable our development of new applications.

I am also working on a personal project called Nobody Wants Your Sheep. It is a real-time Settlers of Catan browser game, built with React, Redux, Firebase, Canvas API and others.
