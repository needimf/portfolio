About Me

I’m a Web Developer working in JavaScript, MERN Stack, Ruby on Rails, Python, HTML and CSS. Before I was a Developer I was an undergraduate student at Claremont McKenna College (CMC), and a Head Consultant at CMC’s Center for Writing and Public Discourse (CWPD). As a Head Consultant at the CWPD, I led, managed, and supported a team of 20 Writing Consultants as I prepared and implemented the CWPD’s local outreach program in Pomona, California. Currently, I’m working on Space Strike, a full-stack, real-time battleship browser game, using React.js, Socket.io, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB/Mongoose.

Space Strike is an interstellar rendition of the classic game, Battleship! Players are paired up against other users, taking turns firing missiles at each other's spacecraft. The player who destroys all of their opponent's spacecraft first is declared the winner!

Let's Fly
JavaScript | Node.js | Express | MongoDB/Mongoose

This easy to use app allows travelers to book flights on the fly! Using the robust Amadeus API, Let's Fly helps users find flights for their upcoming trips, and can even suggest destinations for users looking for a new vacation destination. Consumes the Amadeus API, 500px API, Google API, and Wiki API.
